# 📊Superstore Sales Analysis Dashboard - Microsoft Excel

## 📌Project Overveiw

This project is an interactive **Superstore Sales Analysis** Dashboard developed in **Microsoft Excel** to analyze sales performance across different regions, categories, customer segments, and shipping modes. The dashboard transforms raw sales data into meaningful business insights through interactive visualizations and KPI reporting.


## 📈Key Business Insights

 - **Overall Business Performance**: The Superstore generated **Total Sales** of 5,820,182 from 9,994 orders, with an **Average Order Value** of 582.

- **Top Performing Region**: The West region recorded the **highest number** of transactions (3,203), while the South region had the **lowest** (1,620).

- **Top Performing City**: New York City generated the **highest sales** (654,439), followed by Los Angeles, Philadelphia, Seattle, and San Francisco.

- **Customer Segment Performance**: The Consumer segment purchased the **highest quantity** (13,030), outperforming the Corporate and Home Office segments.

- **Category Performance**: Office Supplies recorded the **highest number** of transactions (6,026), followed by Furniture (2,121) and Technology (1,847), making it the most frequently purchased product category.

## ⚙️Technical Features

- **Interactive Slicers:** Region, Year, and Category filters for dynamic dashboard exploration.
- **KPI Dashboard:** Displays Total Sales, Average Order Value, Total Orders, and Quantity Sold.
- **Ship Mode Analysis:** Visualizes sales distribution across different shipping methods.
- **Regional Analysis:** Compares transaction performance across different regions.
- **Monthly Trend Analysis:** Tracks monthly quantity sold to identify sales patterns.
- **Category & Customer Insights:** Displays category-wise and customer segment-wise sales performance.
- **Pivot Tables & Pivot Charts:** Used for efficient data summarization and interactive reporting.
